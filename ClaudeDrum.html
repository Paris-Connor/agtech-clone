🥁 Drum Machine

Click the squares to create your beat pattern!

120 BPM

How to Use:

  • Click squares to activate beats in the pattern
  • Press Play to hear your rhythm loop
  • Adjust tempo to change the speed (60-180 BPM)
  • Mix different drums to create complex rhythms

Music Theory Tip: The grid represents 16th notes in a measure of 4/4 time. Each column is one 16th note. Four beats make one quarter note, which is one beat in common time. Try starting with a simple kick-snare pattern on beats 1 and 3!
